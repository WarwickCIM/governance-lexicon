
## Organisations
- **Derechos Digitales**: Latin America-focused digital rights organization working on internet governance, surveillance, and data protection across the region, with particular attention to marginalized communities. [derechosdigitales.org](https://www.derechosdigitales.org/en/)
- **Electronic Frontier Foundation (EFF)**: Founded in 1990, EFF defends civil liberties in the digital world through litigation, policy advocacy, and technology development. Works on surveillance, encryption, net neutrality, and user privacy rights. [eff.org](https://www.eff.org)
- **Privacy International**: UK-based NGO fighting government and corporate surveillance globally. Conducts legal challenges, research and advocacy on data exploitation, government surveillance, and corporate data practices. [privacyinternational.org](https://privacyinternational.org)
- **Cities Coalition for Digital Rights**: Network of cities committed to upholding digital rights at the local level — including privacy, algorithmic transparency, and participatory democracy. Works with civil society and private sector to develop open, ethical digital infrastructure. [citiesfordigitalrights.org](https://citiesfordigitalrights.org)
- **Ranking Digital Rights (RDR)**: Promotes freedom of expression and privacy by ranking the world's most powerful digital platforms and telecoms companies against human rights standards. Works with advocates, investors, and policymakers to drive corporate accountability. [rankingdigitalrights.org](https://rankingdigitalrights.org)
